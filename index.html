Hey!
My name is Amit Sahoo.

I am a FreeLance Photographer,
and the owner of Lens and Light.

I used to love clicking, from my childhood only holding camera was a great fun. By the time digital world revolutionised the art of clicking moments I had a small Digi cam in my hands, without being worried about the number of photos can be taken and camera rolls to be put, I started my saga of clicking. Initially, it was great fun capturing moments, people, beautiful scenery and everything around. Slowly perspective seeped in , I started taking photos with a feeling and sense. with the passage of time I wanted my photos to speak, I wanted my photos to tell a story. That's how my interest, my passion , my hobby became my new found everlasting love. I started taking photos of everything that was a treat to the eyes,slowly started understanding the details of photography. The art involved was great, techniques were not easy, mastering was a task seemed totally impossible. All that and much more made it more fun to learn. Photography became a way for me to express my feelings, my thoughts, my emotions. Finally, I found something that I could call my own, it became a voice of my Heart. Till, date the only thing I have achieved in this arena is a channel, a path, a window to express and provide freedom to my thoughts. It's a forum where I feel completely relaxed and free. Lastly, I can just say that after I click and see the photo in the digital screen it brings me happiness, peace,solace and the thought of the picture bringing happiness to many other just get me excited to click another one ...rather a better one.

I also have a M.Sc in BioScience from Sri Satya Sai Institute of Higher Learning, Bangalore.
